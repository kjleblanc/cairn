## Prepare

Prepare is AI-only work with no live risky effect. It may include:

- analysis, design, and documentation;
- isolated prototypes that do not become an active default;
- synthetic data and clearly fake credential canaries;
- offline tests, static checks, and local rehearsals; and
- local task files and local commits that do not change authoritative policy,
  defaults, runtime behavior, production, or external systems.

Prepare may reach `DONE` without an experienced human when the approved scope and
checks are complete and no live risky action occurs.

`Prepare: DONE` means only that the offline candidate was completed and checked. It
does not mean the candidate is correct, adopted, safe for live use, or authorized
for Activate. Passing synthetic checks provides Prepare evidence only.

## Activate

Activate begins before any action that would:

- adopt this Draft into Cairn's project contract, canonical framework, defaults,
  or runtime;
- publish, push, release, deploy, distribute, or publicly describe it as active
  policy;
- access, create, inspect, store, use, rotate, or revoke a real credential;
- call an AI provider or another external service;
- incur cost, change billing, authorize spending, or move money;
- affect production, infrastructure, security controls, real users, or real data;
- send messages or write to an external service;
- perform destructive work, make a legal commitment, or cause any other live
  effect; or
- perform safety-critical work.

Every Activate action requires both gates below before it starts:

1. A qualified experienced human appropriate to the affected risk must review the
   exact action and its safeguards.
2. The owner must separately and explicitly approve the exact action, target, cost
   where applicable, and rollback.

The experienced-human gate and exact-owner-approval gate apply to every Activate
category above. Approval of Prepare, acceptance of this Draft, a passing synthetic
rehearsal, or a local commit never authorizes Activate.

After Prepare, the status remains:

> `Prepare: DONE — Activate: NOT AUTHORIZED`
